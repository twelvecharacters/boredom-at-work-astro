Cambodia is Southeast Asia's hidden gem. Angkor Wat alone justifies the trip, but there's so much more: floating villages, pristine beaches, dark history, and genuine hospitality.

Here's how to use AI to plan an unforgettable Cambodia adventure.

## Why Choose Cambodia?

Cambodia offers what many travelers seek:

- **Bucket-list icon** - Angkor Wat is genuinely life-changing
- **Excellent value** - One of Asia's most affordable destinations
- **Uncrowded beaches** - Koh Rong rivals Thailand without the masses
- **Meaningful history** - Khmer Rouge sites are sobering but important
- **Warm people** - Cambodians are famously friendly

### Gluten-Free Challenges

Cambodia is tricky for celiac disease:

```
I have celiac disease traveling in Cambodia.
What's naturally gluten-free in Cambodian cuisine?
What contains hidden gluten?
How do I communicate my needs (Khmer phrase)?
Which restaurants in Siem Reap/Phnom Penh understand gluten-free?
```

**AI insight:** Fish sauce is usually safe. Soy sauce is less common than in other Asian countries but still present. Amok (coconut curry) is often safe. Rice is the staple‚Äîgood news for gluten-free travelers.

### Scam Awareness

```
What should tourists watch out for in Cambodia?
Cover:
- Tuk-tuk pricing
- Temple guide scams
- Fake orphanages (important!)
- ATM and money issues
- Common overcharging

How do I handle each situation?
```

**Critical:** AI correctly flags fake orphanages. Many "orphanages" exploit children for tourist donations. Never visit orphanages as a tourist attraction.
