## The Learning Path

Here's the recommended order:

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1)
1. Read [ChatGPT Tutorial](/chatgpt-tutorial-beginners/)
2. Practice for 2-3 days
3. Start [Google AI Essentials](/google-ai-essentials-review/)

### Phase 2: Depth (Week 2-3)
1. Finish your certificate
2. Read [Claude vs ChatGPT](/claude-vs-chatgpt/)
3. Pick your primary AI tool
4. Read [Prompt Engineering](/prompt-engineering-afternoon/)

### Phase 3: Application (Week 4+)
1. Identify 3 tasks AI can help with
2. Build a workflow
3. Track time saved
4. Share results

## The Bottom Line

You don't need to become an AI expert. You need to become someone who uses AI effectively. That's a much lower bar — and much more valuable in most jobs.

Start with ChatGPT. Take one free course. Pick one tool for your actual work. That's 90% of the value right there.
