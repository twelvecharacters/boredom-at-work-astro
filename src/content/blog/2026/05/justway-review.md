## Justway at a Glance

**Justway is a strong pick if you want one platform for multiple manufacturing methods (3D printing, CNC, sheet metal, injection molding, vacuum casting) at competitive China-based pricing.** It's not the cheapest for pure PCB orders (JLCPCB still wins there), and it's not the fastest for US delivery (Xometry edges it on domestic turnaround). But for hobbyists, makers, and small businesses who want flexibility across processes without juggling four different vendors, it's a credible all-in-one option with a 4.6 out of 5 rating across 1,000+ reviews.

## What Is Justway, and Who Is It For?

Justway is an on-demand manufacturing platform with factory locations in Hangzhou, Shenzhen, and Shanghai. The company operates the standard "upload your CAD file, get an instant quote, place an order" workflow that's become the norm in this space, but it covers a wider range of processes than most competitors.

The five core services are:

- **3D Printing**: FDM, SLA, SLS, MJF, DLP, SLM, plus metal printing in aluminum, stainless steel, titanium, and tool steel
- **CNC Machining**: 3 and 5-axis milling, turning, wire EDM
- **Sheet Metal Fabrication**: laser cutting, bending, welding, punching
- **Injection Molding**: steel tooling with 2-week lead times available, suitable for runs from 1 to 1M parts
- **Vacuum Casting**: silicone molding for small-batch prototypes

Justway is ISO certified, ships to 195+ countries, and provides one-on-one engineering support, which matters when you're sending files for processes you don't fully understand.

## Justway vs PCBWay vs JLCPCB vs Xometry

Most people choosing a manufacturing service end up comparing Justway against three platforms: PCBWay, JLCPCB, and Xometry. Here's the honest breakdown:

**Justway**: Best for multi-process orders and material breadth. Competitive China-based pricing. Build times 3 to 25 days plus international shipping. Ships globally from China.

**PCBWay**: Best for PCB-first projects with small CNC and 3D add-ons. Very cheap for PCB, mid-tier for CNC. 1 to 7 days for PCB; comparable to Justway for CNC. China-based.

**JLCPCB**: Best for the cheapest low-volume PCBs. Cheapest option for PCB; more limited range on CNC and 3D printing. Fast PCB turnaround. China-based.

**Xometry**: Best for US and EU customers wanting domestic turnaround. Pricing typically 2 to 4 times higher than China-based platforms. Faster lead times via US fulfillment options. US-based with a polished UX.

**If you only need PCBs:** Stay with JLCPCB. Their per-board pricing on small runs is the best in the industry, and PCB isn't Justway's primary focus.

**If you want a polished US-based experience and you're willing to pay for it:** Xometry is the safe pick. The UX is more refined, customer support is in your timezone, and US-based fulfillment options exist for time-critical work. But you'll pay 2 to 4 times what a China-based platform charges.

**If you want broad manufacturing capabilities at a reasonable price:** This is where Justway makes sense. The 5-service breadth means you can run a project that needs both a 3D-printed housing and a CNC-machined backplate without splitting orders. PCBWay does this too, but Justway tends to be slightly more focused on the non-PCB side.

## What Justway Does Well

**The breadth of services is genuinely useful.** A lot of real projects need more than one process, a 3D-printed prototype to test fit, then CNC-machined parts for the production version, then a sheet metal enclosure. Doing all three through one vendor means one shipping consolidation, one customer support contact, one set of file standards. That's worth real money.

**Material range is wide.** 100+ materials listed online, including engineering plastics most platforms skip (PEEK, PTFE, FR4, carbon fiber composites). For metal printing you've got the full set, aluminum, stainless, titanium, tool steel.

**The automatic design checks save costly mistakes.** Pre-production warnings on geometry issues mean you don't pay for a part that would have failed regardless of how well the machine ran.

**Real-time production tracking.** You can see where your order actually is in the queue, not just "in production." This is more useful than it sounds, when you're waiting on a part you can plan around it instead of guessing.

**Tolerance is real, not aspirational.** Specs of "down to 0.05mm" for CNC and "±0.1 mm" for sheet metal hold up in practice from the reviews I've seen. That's tighter than you'll get from most hobby-grade options.

## Where Justway Falls Short

I'm not going to pretend Justway is perfect, that would defeat the whole point of an honest review.

**Customer support runs on GMT+8.** If you're in the US or Europe, that means your back-and-forth with engineering support can stretch over 24-hour cycles. It's fine for non-urgent work, but if you have a question that needs an immediate answer, you'll wait until the next business day in China.

**International shipping adds time and cost.** International DHL is fast as international shipping goes, but you're still adding 3 to 7 days on top of build time, plus customs in some countries. If you absolutely need a part in 5 days in the US, Xometry's domestic options will beat Justway every time.

**Communication can occasionally feel scripted.** Customer service is responsive, but some interactions feel template-driven rather than conversational. This isn't unique to Justway, it's true of most China-based platforms, but it's worth knowing if you expect Xometry-style polish.

**Pricing on PCBs isn't competitive.** If you only need printed circuit boards, JLCPCB or PCBWay will beat Justway on price. Justway's strength is in the broader manufacturing services, not in commodity PCB production.
