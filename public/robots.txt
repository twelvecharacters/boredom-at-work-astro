# /tags/ and /archive/ are NOT disallowed — they use on-page noindex, so
# crawlers must be able to reach them to honor it (blocking crawl hides the
# noindex, and would also block the intentionally-indexable tag hub pages).
# Paginated blog pages (/blog/2/ etc.) are noindexed via meta tags.
# They MUST NOT be blocked in robots.txt, otherwise crawlers cannot follow
# the links to older articles (leading to orphan pages and traffic drops).
