===================
THE 5 LEVELS
===================
Level 1: AI Aware — Understand what AI is (1-2 weeks)
Level 2: AI User — Use AI for daily tasks (2-4 weeks)
Level 3: AI Power User — Optimize workflows (1-2 months)
Level 4: AI Builder — Create AI solutions (3-6 months)
Level 5: AI Expert — Lead AI initiatives (6+ months)

Most professionals only need Level 2 or 3!

===================
30-DAY QUICK START
===================
Week 1: Create accounts, tutorials, first tasks
Week 2: Pick main tool, learn prompting, build habit
Week 3: Expand to writing, research, data
Week 4: Custom instructions, workflows, document wins
